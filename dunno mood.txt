<h1 style="font-size:30px; color: brown; text-align:center; line-height:10px; margin-top: 20px;"> MUSIC IS A MOOD </h1>

 

<p style="font-size:20px; color: brown; text-align:center; line-height: 30px; margin-bottom: 20px;">

<b>The mood of a song can be described in many ways. A song can be described as happy, sad, hopeful, lively, dark, epic, intense, and many more. <br>
A mood is the feeling that you feel when you listen to music. It isn’t always about what mood it makes you think of, 
<br>but what mood it actually puts you in. Many songs have moods ranging from happy to sad and lonely to hopeful.</b></p>
